ABOUT US......

Travelers are always excited about experiencing a new place and its people. Royal Bombay Tour offers personalized tours of Mumbai and lets you experience our home as we see it. We help you to explore this dazzling metropolis in all its true colors, its fascinating rituals & traditions and all the happy smiling faces.

Mumbai is the capital of the state Maharashtra, the financial capital of India, home to Bollywood and Marathi film Industry, home to financial institutions such as the Reserve Bank of India, the Bombay Stock Exchange, the National Stock Exchange of India, nuclear institutes like BARC, NPCL, IREL, TIFR, AERB, AECI, and the Department of Atomic Energy..

Oh !! And did we mention Mumbai is home to about 18 million people, embracing different religious background like Hinduism, Muslim, Jainism, Buddhism, Christianity, Parsis (Zoroastrians), Sikhs, Jews, making the city a melting pot of many communities and cultures.
